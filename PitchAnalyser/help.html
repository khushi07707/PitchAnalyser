Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI analyze my pitch deck?
PitchPilotAI uses a multi-stage AI pipeline. First, we extract all text, images, and charts from your PDF or PPT using OCR and NLP. Then, specialized AI models analyze each component: market size, business model, team credentials, financial data, competitive landscape, and risk factors. Finally, a composite model generates your VC Score (0-100) and investment recommendation. The entire process takes 30–90 seconds.
What file formats does PitchPilotAI support?
We support PDF, PPT, and PPTX formats up to 50MB (approximately 200 slides). For best results, use text-based PDFs rather than scanned image PDFs. We also support direct Google Slides links — just paste the URL and we'll import your deck automatically.
How is the VC Score (0-100) calculated?
The VC Score is a weighted composite across 12 dimensions: Market Opportunity (15%), Team Strength (18%), Product & Technology (12%), Traction & Revenue (18%), Financial Health (10%), Competitive Moat (10%), Revenue Model (8%), Exit Potential (9%). The weights were derived by backtesting against 10,000+ real investment decisions to maximize predictive accuracy. Scores above 75 indicate "Strong Invest", 50-74 "Moderate", below 50 "High Risk".
Is my pitch deck data kept confidential?
Absolutely. All uploaded files are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Your data is never used to train AI models without explicit consent. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Files are stored in isolated per-account storage buckets. Enterprise customers can enable data deletion upon analysis completion.
Can I invite my team to collaborate?
Yes! Pro plan users can invite up to 10 team members to their workspace. Each member can have different roles: Owner, Admin, Analyst, or Viewer. Team members can access all analyses, download reports, and collaborate on investment decisions. Enterprise plans support unlimited team members with SSO integration.
What is included in the Investment Memo?
The AI-generated Investment Memo includes: Executive Summary, Investment Thesis, Business Overview, Problem & Solution, Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM), Competitive Analysis, Financial Summary, Team Analysis, SWOT Analysis, Risk Assessment, Due Diligence Checklist, and Final Recommendation. Memos are exported as formatted PDF or Word documents.
How many analyses do I get per month?
Free plan: 5 analyses/month. Pro plan: Unlimited analyses. Startup plan: 25 analyses/month. Enterprise: Unlimited + custom limits. All plans include full VC scoring, risk analysis, and report generation. Analysis limits reset on the 1st of each month.
