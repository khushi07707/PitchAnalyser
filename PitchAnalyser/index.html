❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does PitchPilotAI support?
PitchPilotAI supports PDF, PPT, and PPTX files up to 50MB. You can also provide a Google Slides link or Notion page URL. Our AI processes up to 200 slides per deck.
How accurate is the AI analysis?
Our AI has been validated against 10,000+ real VC investment decisions and achieves 94% accuracy on VC score prediction. The system improves continuously with feedback from our investor community.
Is my data secure and confidential?
Absolutely. All uploaded pitch decks are encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256. We never share, sell, or train on your data. Enterprise plans include DPA agreements and SOC 2 Type II compliance.
How long does analysis take?
Most pitch decks are analyzed within 45–90 seconds. Complex decks with extensive financials may take up to 3 minutes. You'll receive a notification when your analysis is ready.
Can I collaborate with my team?
Yes! Pro plans include up to 5 team members. Enterprise plans support unlimited users with role-based permissions, shared workspaces, and inline commenting on analyses.
